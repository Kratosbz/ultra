How We Protect Your Funds

Multiple layers of protection ensure your capital is always safe and accessible.

Segregated Client Funds

Your funds are held in dedicated segregated accounts at top-tier banks, completely separate from our operational funds. Your money can never be used for company expenses.

256-bit SSL Encryption

All data transmitted between you and ultraplustrading is protected by bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption. Your personal information and trading data is always secure.

Negative Balance Protection

We guarantee that your account balance can never fall below zero. Even in extreme market volatility, your maximum loss is limited to your deposited funds.

Two-Factor Authentication

Protect your account with 2FA using authenticator apps or SMS codes. Even if your password is compromised, your account remains secure.

PCI DSS Compliant

We comply fully with Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards. Your card details are never stored on our systems and are processed by certified payment processors.

24/7 Fraud Monitoring

Our automated systems monitor all account activity around the clock for suspicious behaviour. Unusual activity triggers immediate alerts and account protection protocols.
